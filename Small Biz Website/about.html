Welcome to my website!

Hello, my name is Morgan, but my friends call me Mo. I was born in Provo, Utah and soon thereafter moved to the Richfield, Utah area where I lived for 13 years. I grew up learning to love adventure, the outdoors, and how fun photography can be. After the 13 years, I moved to Spanish Fork, Utah where I attended a charter school. Here, by the guidance of a fantstic teacher, I was taught to love learning and how to work hard. I later transferred to Spanish Fork High School where I took my first offical photography class. Suddenly, the camera in my small hands, was a whole new world. I learned to veiw the world as a photographer. Then, finally, my last move and transfer to Salem was where I settled my future. Photography provieded the beauty of my past, captures my present, and provides a future. Why not let my love and passion become my job, too? So, here I am, a humble and happy teenager ready to help you capture your happiness,too.
